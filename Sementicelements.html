facts about cats

Unlike dogs, cats do not have a sweet tooth. When a cat chases its prey, it keeps its head level. A group of cats is called a “clowder”. Cats make about 100 different sounds. Cats have over 30 muscles that control their ears. Cats sleep 70% of their lives. Cats can run at a speed of about 30 mph for short bursts. Cats spend as much as 50% of their awake time grooming themselves.

Cats are meat eaters. Cats spend most of their day sleeping and grooming. Cats have a stronger sense of smell than dogs. Cats can jump very high. Cats can have up to 12 kittens in one litter. Cats come in many colors and patterns. Cats have 18 toes. Cats age much faster than humans.
